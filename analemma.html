Analemma

Welcome to Analemma

This app draws the analemma — the figure-8 path the Sun traces in the sky when you photograph it at the same clock time every day for a year. Today's Sun position is highlighted on the year's curve, and you can scrub through the year to watch it move.

To draw it for your sky, the app needs your location. Nothing is sent anywhere — coordinates stay on your device.
