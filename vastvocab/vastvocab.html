VastVocab Word Challenge

Goal: Guess 5 words using the fewest letters possible.

Gameplay

  • Each word shows its first letter
  • Click alphabet letters to reveal them in all words
  • Type your guess and press Enter
  • Correct words appear in green

Limits

  • 10 letters max - then only guessing allowed
  • 3 wrong guesses - game over
  • Timer - set your preferred duration
  • Category reveal - costs 2 letters

Scoring

Final score = Letters used + (3 √ó wrong guesses) + category cost

Lower scores are better.

Categories

Each curriculum has 100 categories. Each category appears once until all are played.
