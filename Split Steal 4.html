How it works

  1. Every player starts with $10.
  2. Each round, players are randomly paired. The same pair is never matched two rounds in a row, and no player is ever left out.
  3. Each pair's result is one of:
    • Both Split — both keep their money.
    • One Steals — the stealer takes both stacks; the other is eliminated.
    • Both Steal — both are eliminated; their money goes into the pot.
  4. If there's an odd number of players, one auto-advances (never the same person twice in a row).
  5. Pot money (from mutual steals) is shared evenly among the survivors at the start of the next round.
  6. Reaching $40 qualifies a player for the charity donation.
  7. Final showdown: play continues until two players remain and face off:
    • Both Split → they share the pot.
    • One Steals → the stealer wins everything.
    • Both Steal → the pot is lost and nobody wins.
    A lone survivor wins the whole pot.
