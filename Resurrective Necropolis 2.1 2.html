About This Memorial

This work recognizes the diversity of human naming across all cultures. It acknowledges that names carry deep meaning. They can be sacred, earned through life experiences, changed to reflect identity, or held in silence to honor the dead. Some names are visual, some are whistled, some contain sounds unique to specific languages. Every name represents a life, a story, a connection to family and culture.

By speaking these names or respectfully acknowledging when they cannot be spoken, we honor the universal human practice of remembering through naming.
