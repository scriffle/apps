How to Play

Goal: Determine if all the inner colored squares are the same color or if one is different.

Challenge: The surrounding colors create an optical illusion that affects how you perceive the inner colors. This is called "simultaneous contrast."

How it works:

• Choose your difficulty level (2, 3, or 4 blocks)

• Look at the small squares inside the larger colored blocks

• Click "Both Same" (or "All Same") if you think they're identical

• Click "Different" if you think at least one is different

• The outer colors fade to black to reveal the truth!

Tip: Try to ignore the outer colors and focus only on the inner squares. Your response time is being tracked!
