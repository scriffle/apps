Tap a cell to read its half-life, what it emits, and the chain it follows down to a stable daughter. Flick the kitchen light off to watch the drawer decay.

The Dark Decay Principle. Decay occurs only without light, which is why no one has caught a teaspoon multiplying or a mug cracking: observation freezes the nucleus. Alpha and beta emission deposit grime, gamma delivers dents and breaks. The dishwasher, sealed and unlit, is a reactor core.
