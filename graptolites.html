🎯 Extracting Student Specimens

🪶 The Art of Flake Selection

Thin Flakes

2-5mm thick

Palm-sized

Easy to handle

Clean edges

Safe for students

Visible fossils

Clear graptolites

⚡ Flake Shearing Technique

  • Find natural separation layers
  • Insert chisel at shallow angle
  • Gentle twisting motion - not hammering!
  • Listen for the "pop" sound
  • Each flake reveals new fossils!

💎 Quality Control

  • ✅ Each flake inspected for fossils
  • ✅ Sharp edges smoothed
  • ✅ Thickness perfect for light box viewing
  • ✅ Labeled with location data
  • ✅ Ready for student discovery!

Pro Tip: Thin flakes can be held up to light to reveal hidden fossil details - like nature's X-ray!
