The Joule

Newton × Meter = Joule
kg × m ÷ s ÷ s × m = kg × m × m ÷ s ÷ s
Force × Distance = Energy

One Joule: the energy to push with one Newton of force through one meter of distance.

Roughly the energy needed to lift an apple one meter high.
