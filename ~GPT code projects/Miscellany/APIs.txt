There are dozens of high-quality, free (with limits) API services you can integrate into a web app. Here’s a curated list of the most useful, fun, and educational free APIs—especially those that work well for location-based, learning, or public-interest apps (like your Wikipedia explorer):

Geography & Location
OpenStreetMap / Nominatim: Free geocoding/reverse geocoding, map tiles (Nominatim API).

Open-Meteo: Free weather forecasts, no API key needed (open-meteo.com).

GeoNames: Place name search, timezone lookup (geonames.org/export/web-services.html).

IPinfo.io: Free IP geolocation for your users (limit: 50k/month) (ipinfo.io).

Transport APIs: Many public transport networks have free endpoints, e.g., PTV (Victoria, Australia).

Culture, Knowledge & News
Wikidata: Query structured data about people, places, topics (wikidata.org/w/api.php).

NewsAPI: Free tier (100 requests/day) for global news headlines (newsapi.org).

Europeana: Art, museum objects, cultural heritage across Europe (Europeana API).

Astronomy & Space
NASA APIs: Astronomy Picture of the Day, Mars photos, exoplanets (api.nasa.gov).

Open Notify: ISS location, number of people in space (open-notify.org).

Science, Nature, Environment
OpenAQ: Global air quality and pollution data (openaq.org).

GBIF: Global biodiversity/species occurrence (GBIF API).

REST Countries: Country facts (restcountries.com).

Trefle.io: Plant database (limited free tier, needs key) (trefle.io).

Maps & Cartography
OpenStreetMap Tiles: For small-volume public apps (osm.org).

Mapbox (limited free tier): Interactive maps (mapbox.com).

OpenRouteService: Routing and geocoding (openrouteservice.org).

Fun, Quotes, Media
The Cat API / The Dog API: Random cat or dog images (thecatapi.com, thedogapi.com).

Quotes REST API: Inspirational or famous quotes (zenquotes.io).

JokeAPI: Programming, general, or dark jokes (jokeapi.dev).

Educational & Miscellaneous
Open Trivia Database: Trivia questions (opentdb.com).

Dictionary API: Word definitions (dictionaryapi.dev).

Open Library: Books, authors, covers (openlibrary.org/developers/api).

Random User Generator: Fake users for demo/testing (randomuser.me).

Numbers API: Facts about numbers (numbersapi.com).
