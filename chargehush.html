How to Use

Getting Started

Click the Hush button to start monitoring classroom noise. The app will charge the battery when students are quiet (below threshold) and drain it when they're too loud.

Visual Elements

  • Sound Meter (left) - Shows current noise level in real-time (0-10 scale)
  • Battery - Fills with purple when charging. Light grey shows elapsed time.
  • Rolling Graph - Historical noise levels. Green bars = below threshold, red bars = above threshold
  • Status - Shows "Charging" (green) or "Draining" (red)

Settings

  • Activity Time - How long the activity should last (1-120 minutes)
  • Sensitivity - Adjusts microphone sensitivity for your room
  • Noise Threshold - The cutoff between quiet (charging) and loud (draining)
  • Averaging Window - How many seconds to average noise over (2-10s)

Charging & Draining

When noise is below threshold: Battery charges at 1 unit/second
When noise is above threshold: Battery drains at 2 units/second

Completion

When the battery reaches 100%, it automatically triggers a celebratory burn animation with flame emojis, followed by a peaceful "Chillin'" mode with a soft yellow glow.

Teacher Controls

  • B key - Secret boost: doubles charging speed for 5 seconds
  • Release Hush - Manually end the session with burn animation
  • Compact View - Toggle between full and compact layouts
