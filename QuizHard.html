The format (based on the ABC's Hard Quiz)

    

      
RoundFormatHow it runs

      
        
Expert RoundOpen-ended + StealEach of 4 houses gets 5 Qs on its own topic. +5 / −5; steal +10. No elimination.

        
Thom's RoundMultiple choice (only MC round)5 Qs on Thom's secret topic; Q5 has 6 options & double points. Lowest out → "Too Soft" (4→3).

        
People's RoundOpen-ended, against the clockRapid general knowledge, first to buzz. Lowest out (3→2).

        
Hard-OffBinary tiebreakerTwo options; correct eliminates the opponent, wrong eliminates the buzzer.

        
Head-to-Head FinalOpen-ended2 finalist houses alternate harder Qs on their own topics, best-of-five, then a numeric "Thom's Tiebreaker".

      
    

    
Prize: the big brass mug. Sign-off: "Thanks for playing, hard." Multiple-choice questions follow the Haladyna best-practice rules, embedded into the Thom's-Round section of the prompt.
