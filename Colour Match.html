üåà Light Mixing (RGB)

In RGB mode, colors add together like light:

  • Red + Green = Yellow
  • Red + Blue = Magenta
  • Green + Blue = Cyan
  • All three = White

üé® Pigment Mixing (CMY)

In CMY mode, colors subtract like paint:

  • Cyan + Magenta = Blue
  • Cyan + Yellow = Green
  • Magenta + Yellow = Red
  • All three = Black
