Welcome to the Dimensional Ladder 🪜

Every rung pairs two worlds. You manipulate an object in the higher dimension (top panel); the bottom panel shows everything a being one dimension down can perceive — only a slice (cross-section) or a shadow of it.

Start in Flatland — 2D ↔ 1D — then climb the tabs: 2D↔3D (a sphere becomes a circle, a cube a hexagon), 3D↔4D (a tesseract's cube-in-a-cube), and 4D↔5D, where we run out of room to draw — so the extra axes become colour and brightness.
