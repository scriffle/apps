# KEY FORMULAS AND EQUATIONS

## Thermochemistry
- q = mcΔT (heat energy)
- ΔH = negative (exothermic), positive (endothermic)
- Efficiency = (useful energy out / total energy in) × 100%

## Photosynthesis
6CO₂(g) + 6H₂O(l) → C₆H₁₂O₆(aq) + 6O₂(g)

## Cellular Respiration
C₆H₁₂O₆(aq) + 6O₂(g) → 6CO₂(g) + 6H₂O(l)

## Fermentation
C₆H₁₂O₆(aq) → 2C₂H₅OH(aq) + 2CO₂(g)

## Electrochemistry
- Faraday's First Law: m = (MIt)/(nF)
- F = 96,485 C mol⁻¹

## Equilibrium
- K = [products]/[reactants] (with appropriate powers)
- Q vs K determines reaction direction

## Organic Chemistry
- Atom economy = (mass of desired product / mass of all reactants) × 100%
- Percentage yield = (actual yield / theoretical yield) × 100%
