Reformulated Newton's Laws

First Law (Law of Inertia):
A mass maintains its state of motion unless it interacts with another mass.
Second Law (Law of Interaction):
When two masses interact: F₁₂ = -F₂₁ = m₁a₁ = -m₂a₂
Third Law (Law of Simultaneous Action):
All forces exist as simultaneous equal and opposite pairs of the same type between two masses.
